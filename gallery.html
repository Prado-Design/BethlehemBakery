Our Slow-Fermented Levain Breads

Country Sourdough

Our signature loaf, made with organic wheat flour and our 100-year-old levain starter. Crispy crust with a tender, open crumb. Perfect for everything from morning toast to evening sandwiches.

Whole Wheat Levain

Hearty and nutritious, made with freshly milled whole wheat from local organic farms. Rich, nutty flavor with a moist crumb that stays fresh for days.

Seeded Multigrain

A wholesome blend of organic grains topped with sunflower, pumpkin, and sesame seeds. Each slice is packed with flavor and nutrition.

Rustic Baguette

Light and airy with a golden, crackling crust. Our slow-fermented baguette has incredible depth of flavor. Best enjoyed fresh on the day it's baked.

Olive Rosemary

Studded with Kalamata olives and fresh rosemary from our church garden. A savory loaf that pairs beautifully with cheese or makes an excellent accompaniment to soup.

Rye Levain

Dense and flavorful with a distinctive tang. Made with organic rye flour and caraway seeds. A traditional loaf perfect for hearty sandwiches.
