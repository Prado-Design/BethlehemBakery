Local Partners We Support

Green Valley Organic Farm

Our primary source for organic wheat and rye. Family-owned for three generations, committed to sustainable farming practices.

Riverside Dairy Co-op

Providing fresh, local butter and cream. A collective of small family farms dedicated to ethical dairy production.

Church Community Garden

Fresh herbs and seasonal produce grown by our church members. All proceeds support local food banks.

Honey Haven Apiary

Raw, unfiltered local honey from bees that pollinate our community gardens. Supporting local biodiversity.

Mill Stone Grains

Stone-milling our flour fresh daily. Preserving traditional methods and maximum nutrition from every grain.

Heritage Seed Collective

Supplying heirloom grain varieties. Dedicated to preserving agricultural heritage and flavor diversity.
